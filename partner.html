PARTNER & SPONSOR


LBL Philanthropy is committed to building mutually beneficial relationships with organisations over the long term. We offer a range of programs to enable involvement in, and support of, our activities. These include Event sponsorship ,Organisational support through financial donations and Product support through the giving of goods and services that can be used for fundraising purposes. All of our programs include the opportunity for businesses to: achieve brand awareness, involve staff in the delivery of activities and provide other support in the form of financial and product donations.
